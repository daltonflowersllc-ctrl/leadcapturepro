# LeadCapturePro Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

## Introduction
This document outlines the vulnerability disclosure policy for LeadCapturePro. We take security seriously and appreciate your efforts to improve the security of our software.

## Reporting Guidelines
If you discover a security vulnerability in LeadCapturePro, please let us know right away. We ask that you do the following:
- **Make a good faith effort** to avoid violating any laws or regulations while searching for vulnerabilities.
- **Do not exploit the vulnerability** or compromise any data.
- **Provide a detailed report** including steps to reproduce the issue, potential impact, and any other relevant information.

## Contact Information
Please report vulnerabilities to [security@leadcapturepro.com](mailto:security@leadcapturepro.com). We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and aim to provide an update on our progress within a week.

## Responsible Disclosure
We will work to resolve the vulnerability as quickly as we can. If you follow the guidelines set out in this policy, we will not take any legal action against you for your activities. We greatly appreciate your help in keeping LeadCapturePro secure!
